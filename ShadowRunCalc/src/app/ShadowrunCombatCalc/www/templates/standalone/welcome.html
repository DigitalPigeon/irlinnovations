Welcome to DigitalPigeon's Shadowrun Combat Calculator

This tool provides quick calculations for common combat modifiers to determine affects on Attacker and Defender pools, Damage Value (DV), and Armor Penatration (AP)

Select the type of action you'd like to take, and then select all applicable options. Note that you can swipe left and right on most pages to go forward and backwards.
